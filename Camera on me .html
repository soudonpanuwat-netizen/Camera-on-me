Powerful Features

⏱️

Time-Limited Shooting

Choose between 2s, 3s, or 4s clips. Auto-countdown and automatic cut when time's up. Perfect for quick, snappy content.

🎬

Multi-Shot Stitching

Combine multiple takes into one seamless video. Build your perfect vlog one clip at a time with full control.

✨

Beauty Filters

Real-time skin smoothing and soft V-shape effects. LGBTQ+ friendly filters designed to make everyone look their best.

🎨

Draft Buffer

Preview each clip before saving. Delete and retake instantly without losing your entire project.

📱

Landscape Support

Ergonomic layout for horizontal shooting. Buttons positioned for comfortable one-handed operation.

💾

One-Click Export

Combine all clips and save directly to Photos. Professional-grade video quality, instantly shareable.
